\vspace{1em}
\raggedright
\begin{columns}[T]
\column{0.49\textwidth}
\textbf{Limitation of direct merging:}
cross-interaction parameters between elements drawn from distinct
libraries are not defined; manual refitting does not scale to
$\binom{12}{2} = 66$ binary pairs.

\column{0.49\textwidth}
\textbf{Pure machine-learning potentials}
\citep{behler2007,shapeev2016,mishin2021}:
require $10^{4}\!-\!10^{5}$ DFT configurations and forfeit the
physical interpretability of MEAM.
\end{columns}
\end{frame}

The four published libraries are merged into a single 12-element
file; cross-terms are refitted by inverting an NN surrogate of the
MEAM\,$\to$\,$(C_{11}, C_{12})$ map under L-BFGS, with single-element
parameters anchored to DFT references.
