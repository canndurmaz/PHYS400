1. Method recap

For each unordered pair (i,j) the script src/NNIP/dft_reference.py performs a single SCF on a fixed reference structure and computes 

E_form(i,j) = E_mix/N − (n_i · E_bulk_i + n_j · E_bulk_j) / N

where N = ni+nj, and Ebulk_k is the per-atom DFT total energy of pure k taken from the EOS minimum (e_bulk_per_atom). Reference structure: L1₂ when one element is fcc/diamond and the other fcc/hcp/diamond; B2 otherwise.

Lattice parameter is fixed at the unrelaxed average amix = (ai + aj) / 2. Plane-wave cutoff 40 Ry, charge cutoff 320 Ry, MV smearing 0.02 Ry, 6×6×6 Monkhorst–Pack mesh. Spin-polarised (nspin=2) with the initial moments table above whenever Fe, Cr, or Mn is present.

Structural caveat that still applies. The cell is held at amix with no relaxation. For pairs with atomic-volume mismatch >15 % this typically adds +0.2 to +1.0 eV/atom of strain energy to Eform. The L1₂/B2 probe also fails to capture more stable Laves / σ / D0₂₂ / B20 ground states, so the appropriate literature comparison is the same-cell DFT value, not the experimental ground-state ΔHf (both columns are shown below).

6. Remaining recommendations

  1. Add cell relaxation. Pairs with large volume mismatch (Cu-Zn, Cu-Ti, Al-Zn) still sit well above same-cell DFT literature because amix = (ai+aj)/2 is not the equilibrium L1₂/B2 lattice constant. Switching the binary SCF to calculation='vc-relax' should bring >90 % of pairs into the |Δ|<0.10 eV/atom band.
  2. Replace the α-Mn proxy with γ-Mn (fcc, AFM) or Materials Project's mp-35. The current BCC-at-2.89 Å proxy converges, but does not represent the experimental ground state of Mn. A γ-Mn fcc cell with AFM-double-layer ordering would be physically better; alternatively pull Ebulk_Mn from MP for consistency with the rest of the literature table.
  3. Use AB₂ Laves-cell probes for Cu-Mg, Mg-Zn, Cr-Ti, Fe-Mo. These four pairs have well-known Laves ground states (MgCu₂, MgZn₂, TiCr₂, Fe₂Mo) that are very far from a B2 cell. Probing the C14/C15 Laves cell instead of B2 would shrink the |Δ| for those rows to near zero.
  4. Keep the sanity gate. The new _run_binary_pair raises on missing reference; consider additionally rejecting any |Eform| > 2 eV/atom result with a warning so future runs can't silently produce non-physical seeds for MEAM cross-terms.
  5. Regenerate Stage 3 of the interim report. reports/interim/figures/formation_energy_heatmap.png should be rebuilt from the corrected dft_results.json so its colour scale is no longer dominated by ±10³ eV/atom outliers.
