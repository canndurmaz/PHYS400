\begin{frame}{Stage 1: Random Alloy Generator}
\begin{columns}[T]
\column{0.50\textwidth}
A Python generator draws compositions $\{c_i\}$ uniformly on the
simplex $\sum_i c_i = 1$ over the 12 admissible elements, with
\textbf{Al-dominant bias} (Al weight inflated when present)
to oversample the engineering-relevant aluminium-alloy corner.

\medskip
\textbf{Output schema.} Each composition is persisted as a structured
record (element $\to$ mass-fraction map) at $10^{-4}$ precision,
fully consumable by the downstream stages.

\medskip
\textbf{Coverage.} $\sim\!7900$ compositions generated; after the
Stage-2 physicality filter, $7815$ survive.

\end{columns}
\end{frame}
