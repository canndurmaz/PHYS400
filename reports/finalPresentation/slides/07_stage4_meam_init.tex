\column{0.42\textwidth}
\textbf{Screening exponent from the DFT triple
$(a_{0}, E_{\mathrm{coh}}, B)$:}
\begin{equation*}
  \alpha = a_{0}\,\sqrt{\dfrac{9\,B\,\Omega}{E_{\mathrm{coh}}}}
\end{equation*}

\smallskip
$\Omega$: primitive-cell volume.

\bigskip
Together with the DFT-seeded cross-term energies $E_{c}(i, j)$,
these constants yield a complete 12-element MEAM library suitable
for direct evaluation in LAMMPS.

\bigskip
\scriptsize\textit{Computational cost: $\sim\!70$\,ms once DFT
references are cached. The $\alpha$-Mn equation of state did not
converge; a relaxed BCC approximation is used.}
